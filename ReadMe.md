Take control of your scrolling habit with YScroll.

YScroll is a simple, easy-to-use Chrome extension designed to help you stay productive by limiting time spent on short-form content like YouTube Shorts and social feeds. Endless scrolling can steal hours from your day — YScroll gives you back control of your time.

Why use YScroll?

- Stop losing hours to addictive short videos

- Build better focus habits

- Track your progress 

How it works:

- Set your daily limit – Decide how many Shorts or minutes you want to watch each day.

- Smart reminders – Get notified when you reach your limit, so you don’t go over.

- Progress tracking – See your progress.

- Optional lock or redirect – Once you hit your limit, YScroll can redirect you to a productive page or block further Shorts automatically.

YScroll is lightweight, easy to install, and works seamlessly in the background. Stay focused, stay productive, and take control of your online time — without missing the content you enjoy.
