Usage Data: YScroll tracks your scrolling behavior on supported sites (e.g., number of posts/Shorts viewed, time spent scrolling) to provide feedback, notifications, and streak tracking. This data is stored locally in your browser.

Optional Future Data (if users opt in):
Personally Identifiable Information (PII): Name and email for account features like progress syncing or backup.
Authentication Information: Credentials for optional account features.
